JONNY EVANS SIGNS FOR UNITED
Manchester United is pleased to confirm that Jonny Evans has joined the club, signing a one-year contract until June 2024.
RASMUS HOJLUND SIGNS FOR UNITED
Manchester United have confirmed the signing of Rasmus Hjlund from Bod/Glimt on a four-year contract. The 21-year-old striker is United's first signing of the summer transfer window.
SOFYAN AMRABAT SIGNS FOR UNITED
Manchester United have signed Sofyan Amrabat from Fiorentina on a season-long loan with an option to buy. The 27-year-old midfielder is United's seventh summer signing.
MASON MOUNT SIGNS FOR UNITED
There have been some rumors that United are interested in signing Mount, but there is no concrete evidence to support these rumors. It is more likely that United will focus on other targets in the summer transfer window.
DAVID DEGEA LEAVE FOR UNITED
David de Gea is no longer a Manchester United player. His contract expired on June 30, 2023, and he has not yet signed a new contract with the club. He is currently a free agent and is free to join any club he wants.
ANDRE ONANA SIGNS FOR UNITED
Manchester United have signed Andre Onana from Inter Milan on a five-year contract. The 27-year-old goalkeeper is United's second signing of the summer transfer window.
SERGIO REGUILON SIGNS FOR UNITED
Manchester United have signed Sergio Reguilon from Tottenham Hotspur on a season-long loan with no option to buy. The 26-year-old left-back is United's sixth summer signing.
ALTAY BAYINDIR SIGNS FOR UNITED
Manchester United have signed Altay Bayindir from Fenerbahce on a four-year contract with the option of a further year. The 25-year-old goalkeeper is United's fourth summer signing.
